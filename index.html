About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at MMLab@UIT, VNUHCM - University of Information Technology (UIT), supervised by Dr. Khang Nguyen and Prof. Tam Nguyen. I got my Master's degree in computer science from, The VNUHCM-University of Science (VNU-HCMUS). My research interests include Anomaly detection, Image Captioning, Document image understanding, Fashion Retrieval, UAV vehicle ReID.
